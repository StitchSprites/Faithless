- INFO & FAQ -

Q) "This feature looks broken, how do I fix? I have x mods."
A) First check out the shader config file Faithless\assets\minecraft\shaders\include\config.glsl and set any mod values to true if you have the mod enabled

Q) "Will the pack update automatically every time something new is added?"
A) No. It is not automatically updated ingame unfortunately. You will need to re-download the pack

Q) "Can I use this pack for my modpack/video/personal pack/ect..."
A) My answer will always be yes, under the condition credit is properly given with a link to the pack's original download site https://modrinth.com/resourcepack/faithless

Q) "Can you make an items/gui/block/ect... only version? I dont like "x""
A) You may edit the pack yourself if you know how. I dont have plans on breaking up a project I worked hard to put together.

Q) "Why does my version look different than yours?"
A) Could be one of 2 reasons. Either 
		1 - your version is outdated and requires to be redownloaded on the official site : https://modrinth.com/resourcepack/faithless
		2 - You dont have optifine installed, or if you do then some settings may not be set up properly
		
Q) "Where are the custom music disc minecraft parodies?"
A) Those are included in an addon which is exclusive to our discord server at https://discord.gg/nctrgW29ab
